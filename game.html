RUSTED

PLATFORMER


Rusted is an action-adventure platformer game created by a member of Exhibit IV and his teammates. It immerses the player in a story of a robot that was deemed defective by society. He then goes after the ones that sentenced him to his demise once he has repaired himself.

We follow the story of an adorable robot character who is tossed down a chute, leading to a factory where robots get decommissioned, by the illustrious company known as Rusted. Our little robot is an old and inferior model, Methius AR5BXB63, with defective parts.

Double Jump

This part grants the character the ability to jump twice, allowing him to reach higher platforms.

Shoot

This part grants the character the ability to shoot electric bullets at enemies. These will only temporarily disable enemies for a few seconds.

Force Field

This part grants the character the ability to protect himself against bullets, projected from enemies, with a electrical force field that surrounds him.

Ram

This part grants the character the ability to propel himself at enemies, using his horn to deal serious damage.
